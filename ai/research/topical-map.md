- **Difficulty is extremely low (0–23)** across almost all terms — this niche is very winnable for a focused authority site.

- **Geographic long-tail** terms like "hotshot insurance texas" and "hotshot insurance florida" exist but have minimal tracked volume; state-specific landing pages are a future opportunity.
